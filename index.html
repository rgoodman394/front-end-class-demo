ABOUT ME

My name is Bekah Goodman. Coding and photography are two of my greatest passions. Both involve creativity, technical skill, and a whole lot of experience to master. Both can enrich the lives of others in impactful ways. My educational background is in counseling. Diagnosing, asking questions, getting to the root of problems, and making pathways to solutions - all things that I find myself utilizing as a counselor and in coding and photography.

CAMERAS I USE

Cameras, in their most basic form, are containers that collect and preserve the surrounding light at a specific point in time. They are tools, allowing the user to capture memories and people and places and things, in a way that otherwise would be lost forever. I shoot with a combination of film and digital cameras. Each one gives a different feel, but I tend to prefer a vintage, emotive image and each one has their ways of achieving the look. Click on the cameras below to see some of my favorite pictures.
